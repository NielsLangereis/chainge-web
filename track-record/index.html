track record

Every prediction lands in a public ledger.

This is the methodology track record. Every cascade prediction Chainge ships carries a locked horizon, capped at 90 days. When the horizon arrives, the prediction is scored against primary-source data: right, partial, wrong, or unclear. The original text is never edited. The ledger reads as a record, not a story.

The fully rendered ledger is being built. What follows is the current state, openly summarised, with the first verdict shown in context. Come back as horizons land; this page will fill in.
