01 · method

Methodology and architecture

Chainge traces how a distant event, a regulation, a currency move, an export control, travels across industries and lands on one company's specific exposure.

The thesis it runs on. Granovetter's strength of weak ties. The information that matters travels through the loose links between industries, not the obvious ones inside a sector. Any global event connects to a specific local exposure in roughly six steps or fewer, and the value is in finding those steps. This is not decoration; it is the methodological claim that makes the product defensible. A sector report cannot see across its own boundary, and that is exactly where the signal is.

End to end. The system is a pipeline, deliberately simple at the synthesis layer and deep at the data layer.

01 ingest

Public and primary data

RSS for the news layer, then the grounded sources that do the real work: EUR-Lex for EU law, FRED for macro, ENTSO-E for European power, GDELT for geopolitics, public investment-house consensus, EXIOBASE and CBS for supply-chain triangulation. Every downstream claim carries provenance. Depth of ingest, not cleverness of prompt, is the strategy.

02 profile

A structural exposure map

Each subject becomes an exposure map at the right altitude: input category by geography by regulatory regime, never a brittle per-supplier list. Each leg carries a weight, with an explicit upgrade slot for customer-supplied data. That slot is the flywheel.

03 engine

Discover, then ground

A single structured pass generates candidate cross-domain bridges against the full profile, a grounded engine verifies each link, and a skeptic pass judges them. Simple and grounded over a clever multi-lens scaffold, because the evidence said the lift came from data, not architecture.

04 gate

Tier, then skeptic

Among grounded chains, a probability tier sorts headline from watch from demote. Grounding overrides probability: a chain that cannot be evidenced cannot lead, however plausible. Honest failures, missing data, paywalls, ungrounded legs, are surfaced rather than hidden.

05 record

Verdicts accumulate

Every cascade is re-forecast each week and its verdict marked against primary sources as the world resolves. The overlay is cluster-stable, so the public record stays live every week regardless of news volume.

03 · cadence

Operating cadence and autonomy

This is the beyond-hobby answer: an AI-native operating model with a small human core, disciplined exactly where discipline matters.

Chainge has run a live cascade every week since W19. The scoring and resolution loop runs unattended: it backfills, polls, resolves, and monitors as a scheduled stage of the weekly run. That is the autonomy claim, and it is precise. The publish path on any new cluster is deliberately supervised: nothing new reaches a public surface without a human check. The loop is autonomous; the publish is supervised. We do not claim it all runs itself.

  • Publicness gate. Every push through an auditor that catches name-plus-detail combinations before anything reaches the public repo.
  • Supervised hold on new clusters. A new cluster is held for a human check before it can surface. It has already caught combination-level leaks before they went public.
  • Resolution loop. Backfill, poll, resolve, monitor, scheduled into the weekly run and monitored for false positives.
  • Remit gate. The engine traces external exposures reaching a company, never its own solvency or returns. A self-check enforces the boundary.
  • Fail-closed persist. A failed or partial run holds the last good state and says so, rather than shipping a broken or empty public page.
